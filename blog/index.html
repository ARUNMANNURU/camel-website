ROADMAP ⸱ December 31, 2019 ⸱ Claus Ibsen

RELEASE 2.24.3

The Camel community announces the immediate availability of the new patch release Camel 2.24.3. This release contains 25 fixes and improvements applied in the past weeks by the community on the camel-2.24.x branch. It is the last planned patched release for the camel-2.24.x branch. The artifacts are published and ready for you to download either from the Apache mirrors or from the Central Maven repository. For more details please take a look at the release notes.

JBoss Asylum Podcast about Apache Camel 3

JBoss Asylm Podcast episode 47: What do you call an Apache Camel with 3 humps Claus Ibsen and Luca Burgazzoli sits down with Emanual and Max (hosts) and talks about what is coming in Apache Camel 3 on topics like Camel K and Camel Quarkus.
